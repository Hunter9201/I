Drafted by: Lionhunt Legal Services

Republic of Uganda

In the matter of the contract act 2010

And

In the matter of a tenancy agreement (Lutete L.C 1 Village, Wakiso District)

THIS TENANCY AGREEMENT

made this day of 2024.

BETWEEN

ELEANOR ADAMS, Tel: 0781554321 Wakiso
(hereinafter referred to as the first party and landlady) which expression shall where the context so admits include her agents, assignees or successor in title on the one part.

AND

, Tel
(hereinafter referred to as the second party and tenant) which expression shall where the context so admits include his/her agents, assignees or successor in title on the other part.

WITNESSETH AS HEREUNDER:

WHEREAS the landlady/1st party is the proprietor of the above mentioned rental property developed thereon with bedroom(s).

AND WHEREAS the landlady is desirous of letting out the said premises to the tenant/2nd party at a consideration of a monthly rent basis as per the agreed terms and conditions below.

1. CONSIDERATION:
The tenant shall pay to the landlady a monthly rent fee of Ugx payable at the commencement of every month of occupation as the reserved rent for the premises occupied. The tenant shall pay rent at least not exceeding every 5th day of a new month and upon occupation, the landlady shall introduce the tenant to the area local council authorities.
2. TENURE:
Both parties subsequently do hereby agree that this contract shall be for a fixed period of THREE (3) MONTHS payment, but subject to review at the expiry of the initial term, upon conditions as may be agreed thereto and each tenant shall independently cater for their own utility and other related bills.
3. SECURITY DEPOSIT:
The tenant shall deposit extra Ugx (equivalent to 3 months coverage) with the landlady upon signing/execution of this agreement as security deposit which shall be refunded to the former if he/she abides by the conditions of this tenancy at the expiry of the same. The security shall not be refunded to the 2nd party in case of damages and defects to the property caused by him/her on the premises.
4. PAYMENT METHOD:
All such payments as herein prescribed shall, at all times be effected on the landlady's Ugx Bank Account vide 0010341426, held at Tropical Bank (U) Limited, the receipt of which shall be communicated to the 1st party/landlady herein.
5. USER:
The tenant hereby undertakes to strictly utilize the premises for residential purposes only and in event of desire to change purpose of occupation/use, the tenant shall first seek permission/consent from the 1st party/landlady in writing.
REPAIRS:
The landlady will be responsible for major repairs of the premises upon occupation by the tenant and it will be the responsibility of the latter to ensure that the premises are kept in good shape and its natural conditions. In case of damages, he/she will meet the costs of repair of the same as covered by the security per (paragraph 3) above. However, in case there are no alterations or damages to the property by the tenant, the landlady or her agents shall refund his/her security at the termination/expiry of the tenancy.
6. SANITATION:
The tenant undertakes to always keep the premises clean by preserving good hygiene from time to time. At the expiry of the tenancy the tenant shall leave the premises in a good state as he/she found it.
7. SECURITY:
The tenant shall observe discipline by not subjecting the premises to unnecessary noise pollution/public nuisance as this may cause discomfort and denial of quiet possession/enjoyment of the premises by the landlady and other tenants/neighbours thereto.
8. BILLS AND UTILITY:
The tenant shall be responsible for the payment of all bills, utility and garbage (10,000/= per month through the landlady) during the course of their tenancy in the premises.
9. TERMINATION:
Both parties agree that the tenancy shall stand terminated upon either party giving the other at least a 3 (three) Month(s) notice in writing. It will only be sufficient notice if such notice is delivered at the last known address of either party and the same shall be effective from the date of receipt of the same by the other party.
IT IS FURTHER EXPRESSLY AGREED
that at the termination of the tenancy, the tenant shall leave the premises in a good state of repair and shall effect all the necessary repair of the premises, where need be. The tenancy shall stand terminated if the reserved rent is not paid in advance of the agreed period and the landlady may proceed with the distress for rent.
10. MATERIAL ALTERATIONS:
Both parties agree that any material alterations on the set up of the premises shall be by a written consent of the landlady and in so doing, the same shall be carried out at the tenant's expense.
11. UTMOST GOOD FAITH:
The parties to this agreement do agree to be bound by the doctrine of utmost good faith in their contractual relationship and obligations herein.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF
Both parties do affix their hands and signatures on the, year and month above stated and in case of breach of this contract, the same shall be referred to Courts of law for legal interpretation or redress.
SIGNED and delivered by the said; ELEANOR ADAMS
(LANDLADY)
SIGNED and delivered by the said;
(TENANT)
